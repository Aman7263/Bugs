🧠 Emotional Profile by Birth Date

This tool gives you a simple emotional profile based on the day you were born. It does not predict your future and does not require belief in astrology.

Just enter your date of birth.

What does this mean?

When you were born, the Moon was at a specific point in its monthly cycle. People born around the same time tend to develop similar emotional habits.

Your result describes:
  • How you usually process feelings
  • How you react to stress
  • What helps you feel mentally balanced
This is similar to a personality test — it shows tendencies, not fixed destiny.
🧠
How to read this visual:
Center = You
Rings = Emotional stages of the month
Dot = Stage when you were born

The diagram is only a visual aid. It does not represent planets, signs, or predictions.
