🧭 How This Works

Simple explanation — no astrology knowledge needed

🌱 What is this?

This dashboard is a self-understanding tool. It helps you learn about your emotions, habits, and inner nature in a simple way.

It is made to help you reflect and understand yourself, not to control or decide your life.

📅 Why do we ask for your Date of Birth?

Your birth date is used only as a starting reference. Nothing more.

Just like seasons affect nature, your birth time is used here to understand emotional patterns — not your fate.

🌙 What does astrology mean here?

Here, astrology is used only as a symbolic explanation. You do not need to believe in astrology to use this tool.

  • 🌙 Moon → emotions and feelings
  • ♈ Zodiac signs → emotional styles
  • 🔁 Cycles → natural life changes

✅ What this tool helps you with

  • Understanding your emotions
  • Knowing how you react in situations
  • Improving emotional awareness
  • Better understanding in relationships
  • Calm self-reflection

❌ What this tool does NOT do

  • Predict the future
  • Decide success or failure
  • Control your life choices
  • Replace doctors or professionals

Your life always depends on your actions and choices.

🔐 Privacy & Safety

Your data is not stored. Everything happens instantly in your browser and is never saved.
