What is FLAMES?

FLAMES is a traditional way to describe the type of emotional connection between two people, based on name interaction.

Each letter stands for a relationship theme:
  • F — Friendship
  • L — Love
  • A — Affection
  • M — Marriage
  • E — Emotional bond
  • S — Support

This method reflects tendencies, not destiny.
