My Love ❤️

I don’t really know where to start, because whenever I think about you, my heart gets a little too full and my words start running faster than my thoughts.

Somewhere between our first conversation and all those endless talks, you quietly became my favorite person. I didn’t even realize when “you” turned into my comfort, my happiness, and my safe place.

You are the reason my bad days don’t feel so heavy, and my good days feel even better. You somehow manage to make me laugh when I don’t want to smile, and calm me down when my mind refuses to slow down. (Honestly, I still don’t know how you do that.)

I love how we can be silly together, how small moments with you turn into memories, and how even silence feels peaceful when it’s with you. From late night talks to random laughs, from little fights to even bigger love — every part of us feels real and beautiful.

You are not just someone I love, you are someone I choose. Every single day.

If life ever feels confusing or overwhelming, I want you to remember this: you are deeply loved, you matter more than you know, and you will always have me — cheering for you, standing with you, annoying you, and loving you endlessly.

You are my today, my tomorrow, my favorite thought, and my biggest blessing. In short… you are my life, and I wouldn’t want it any other way.

Forever yours,
❤️

Meri Jaan ❤️

Tum meri zindagi ka woh hissa ho jo bina bole sab kuch keh jaata hai. Tumhara hona hi kaafi hota hai cheezon ko theek karne ke liye.

Kahin hamari pehli baat aur un lambe conversations ke beech, tum meri aadat ban gayi. Dheere-dheere, bina shor ke, tum meri sukoon aur meri sabse safe jagah ban gayi.

Tum mere bure din ko halka bana deti ho aur mere ache din ko aur khoobsurat. Tumhari hasi meri favorite awaaz hai, aur tumhara saath meri sabse badi taqat.

Mujhe woh sab pasand hai jo hum saath karte hain — bina wajah hansna, late night baatein, choti si nok-jhok, aur phir ek dusre ko aur zyada samajhna.

Tum sirf meri pasand nahi ho, tum meri choice ho. Har din, har haal mein.

Agar kabhi zindagi bhaari lage, toh bas itna yaad rakhna — tum akeli nahi ho. Main hamesha tumhare saath hoon, pyaar karne ke liye, support karne ke liye, aur thoda sa tang karne ke liye bhi.

Tum meri aaj ho, tum meri kal ho, aur tum hi meri poori duniya ho.

प्रियसि ❤️

त्वं मम जीवनस्य सारः असि। तव स्मरणे एव मम हृदयम् शान्तिं लभते।

यदा त्वं समीपे भवसि, तदा कालः अपि स्थिरः इव दृश्यते। तव सहवासः मम आत्मनः विश्रान्तिः।

न केवलं प्रेम, अपि तु विश्वासः, स्नेहः, च नित्यं वर्धते।

जीवनपथे यावत् अहं गच्छामि, तावत् त्वमेव मम पन्थाः। त्वं मम धैर्यम्, त्वं मम प्रेरणा।

अद्य, श्वः, सर्वदा च — त्वमेव मम हृदयस्थिता।
